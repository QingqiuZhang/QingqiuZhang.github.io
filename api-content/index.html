Normally, the feature with larger range would influence the result more. Nevertheless, this doesn't mean it's more important than the other one. That's why we need scale the features. Frequently used scaling techniques are: MinMaxScaler() StardardScaler() MaxAbsScaler() RobustScaler() I usually choose MinMaxScaler() or StandardScaler() for most of datasets.
