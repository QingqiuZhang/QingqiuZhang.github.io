Plans for October, 2022

Author: Qingqiu Zhang Date: 2022-09-18

1. [Unsupervised ML] K-means Clustering from Scratch

I am thinking about releasing a post about how to do K-means clustering without any ML libraries. I will include both "random" and "kmeans++" initialization. Meanwhile, I will write functions on Silhouette score and Normalized Mutual Information score as well. The optimal number of clusters k is also a key point in this article and I'm considering use elbow method and Silhouette score to decide the k. At last I'll wrap up the post with a comparison between my implementation and sklearn.

2. [Deep Learning] Backpropagation from Scratch

I haven't started this yet but I will definately work on it sometime in the next few weeks. I found there are so many get confused with backpropagation and I really think it's necessary to write an article explaining the mechanism and the implementation in details.
